3.1.4 Closing appliances and ducts
3.1.4.1 Arrangements shall be provided to permit a rapid shutdown and effective closure of the ventilation system from outside of the space in case of fire, taking into account the weather and sea conditions.
Refer to the IACS UI SC243 Access to controls for closing of ventilation of vehicle, special category and ro-ro spaces
Refer to MSC.1/Circ.1434 "UNIFIED INTERPRETATIONS OF SOLAS CHAPTER II-2"
3.1.4.2 Ventilation ducts, including dampers, within a common horizontal zone shall be made of steel. In passenger ships, ventilation ducts that pass through other horizontal zones or machinery spaces shall be "A-60" class steel ducts constructed in accordance with regulations 9.7.2.4.1.1 and 9.7.2.4.1.2. ( Replaced by Res.MSC.365(93))
