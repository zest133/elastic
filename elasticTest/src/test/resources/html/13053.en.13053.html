SECTION VII - OTHER LIFE-SAVING APPLIANCES

Regulation 49

Line-throwing appliances

1 Every line-throwing appliance shall:

.1 be capable of throwing a line with reasonable accuracy;
.2 include not less than four projectiles each capable of carrying the line at least 230m in calm weather;
.3 include not less than four lines each having a breaking strength of not less than 2 kN;
.4 have brief instructions or diagrams clearly illustrating the use of the line-throwing appliance.

2 The rocket, in the case of a pistol fired rocket, or the assembly, in the case of an integral rocket and line, shall be contained in a water-resistant casing. In addition, in the case of a pistol-fired rocket, the line and rockets together with the means of ignition shall be stowed in a container which provides protection from the weather.
