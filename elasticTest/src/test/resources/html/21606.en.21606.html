Part B - Special Provisions for solid  bulk cargoes (Replaced by Res.MSC.269(85))

Regulation 6

Acceptability for shipment

1 Prior to loading a solid (Inserted by Res.MSC.269(85)) bulk cargo, the master shall be in possession of comprehensive information on the ship's stability and on the distribution of cargo for the standard loading conditions. The method of providing such information shall be to the satisfaction of the Administration.*

* Refer to regulation II-1/5-1 on Stability information to be supplied to the master.
