3.1.2 Ceilings and linings
3.1.2.1 In passenger ships, except in cargo spaces, all linings, grounds, draught stops and ceilings shall be of non-combustible material except in mail rooms, baggage rooms, saunas or refrigerated compartments of service spaces. (The last sentence, "Partial bulkheads or decks used to subdivide a space for utility or artistic treatment shall also be of non-combustible materials.", was deleted by Res.MSC.216(82))
